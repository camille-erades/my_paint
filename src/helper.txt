PROJET : my_paint

how to use :

clic on the "my_paint" page on the
top of your screen and use
left clic to draw

in the menu page, interact as follow :
----------
clic [FILE] to save, open or create a new page

clic [EDIT] to change color, size, tool or shape

clic [HELP] to hide the help menu
